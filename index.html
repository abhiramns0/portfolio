Grama: A one stop for rural retail

Grama is a design solution aimed at empowering rural commerce by connecting shopkeepers, customers, suppliers, and delivery agents through a unified platform. By addressing inventory management, pricing transparency, and credit systems, Grama fosters trust, enhances efficiency, and strengthens community bonds in the heart of rural India.
